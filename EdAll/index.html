Latest News & Events

    One More Milestone in the history of IIT Mandi North Campus has become functional

      After the nearly complete establishment of the South Campus of IIT Mandi at Kamand, now the North Campus at Salgi has started to become functional, with the shifting of the Director, few faculty with their family and PG students.

    Congratulations to Deepanshu Rastogi, an IIT Mandi alumnus (Class of 2013), who joined National Power Committee Division of Central Electricity Authority as Assistant Director-I on 19th September, 2016.

      Deepanshu secured AIR-58 in Engineering Services Examination-2015, conducted by UPSC for recruitment of the central government services that meet the technical and managerial functions.

    The Faculty and Staff Recreation Club of IIT Mandi, was inaugurated by Dr. Priscilla Gonsalves in the presence of Prof. Timothy A. Gonsalves (Director, IIT Mandi) on 29th October 2016.

      The club plans to regularly organize music, dance and painting classes for kids and also organise social get-together on national days and festivals.
